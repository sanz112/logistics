About Us!

The Company was established in March 2012, as a result of the Port Reforms initiated by the Nigerian Government, with the aim to provide a high standard logistics service to Port users and Port Terminal Operators. JOATELIM LOGISTICS LIMITED offers its services for shipments by air and sea including logistics for Customs formalities and handles transportation for all your imports to and exports from Nigeria. Our principal areas of activity are shipping agency, empty container holding-bay, import, export, haulage, warehousing and plant service. JOATELIM LOGISTICS LIMITED is able to ensure that it remains at the heart of the Nigerian shipping industry. It has since become one of the most advantageous holding-bay operator that directly located close to the port with 90% capacity above other empty container holding —bay in Lagos, Nigeria We have good working relationship with the national and international communities and our Company have properly positioned itself to meet the challenges of the industry. The Company identifies customers' needs, review services and make value-adding contribution to clients businesses.
