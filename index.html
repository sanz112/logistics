About Us!

The Company was established in March 2012, as a result of the Port Reforms initiated by the Nigerian Government, with the aim to provide a high standard logistics service to Port users and Port Terminal Operators.

JOATELIM LOGISTICS LIMITED offers its services for shipments by air and sea including logistics for Customs formalities and handles transportation for all your imports to and exports from Nigeria. Our principal areas of activity are shipping agency, empty container holding-bay, import, export, haulage, warehousing and plant service.

We have registered with Nigeria Customs Services as one of the Clearing and Forwarding Agency in Nigeria, The Clearing and Forwarding Agency services give us logistics advantage being an indigenous Company to further expand our services to Honeywell Flour Plc and other Nigeria numerous Importers.

JOATELIM SHIPPING AGENCY

The Shipping services give us logistics advantage being an indigenous registered Shipping Company as a result of the Port Reforms initiated by the Nigerian Government, with the aim to providing a high standard service to port users. The office is well equipped IT system and man by dedicated and well trained staff for the best shipping service with a personal touch in order to satisfy the Nigerian importers and port stakeholders.

JOATELIM BONDED TERMINAL.

Our Customs Bonded Terminal services was approved by Nigeria Customs services and it will give logistics advantage being located within the port area, there easy access to vessel in terms of quick evacuation of laden containers . The Import Bonded terminal is well equipped with four 45 tones capacity containers handling plants, IT system, well trained staff and security services is being currently managed by Nigerian Police, Port Command for the best terminal services with a personal touch in order to satisfy the Nigerian importers and port stakeholders.
