About Us!

The Company was established in March 2012, as a result of the Port Reforms initiated by the Nigerian Government, with the aim to provide a high standard logistics service to Port users and Port Terminal Operators.

JOATELIM LOGISTICS LIMITED offers its services for shipments by air and sea including logistics for Customs formalities and handles transportation for all your imports to and exports from Nigeria. Our principal areas of activity are shipping agency, empty container holding-bay, import, export, haulage, warehousing and plant service.
